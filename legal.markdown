# Accessibility Disclaimer (Mississippi Small Business)
#### ⚖️ Accessibility Statement Disclaimer

Taekwondo University is a locally owned and operated small business in Mississippi. While we strive to make our website user-friendly and accessible to all visitors, we are not a government entity, federally funded institution, or organization subject to Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act or the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Title III website accessibility standards.

As a private business, we are not legally required to conform to any specific digital accessibility guidelines such as WCAG 2.1 or related standards. However, we make good-faith efforts to ensure our online content is reasonably accessible to the general public.

If you experience difficulty accessing any portion of our website, please contact us directly at <a href="mailto:brumley@tkduniv.com" style="color: blue; text-decoration: underline;">brumley@tkduniv.com</a>, and we will make every effort to provide the requested information through an alternative method.

# Cookie & Tracking Policy
#### 🍪 Cookie and Data Usage Notice

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website performance, and support marketing efforts. These tools may collect anonymous data such as page views, click behavior, and general geographic location.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Any tracking or analytics information is used solely to improve our marketing effectiveness and website performance.

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

**Continued use of our website indicates consent to our use of cookies as described in this notice.**

#### 📱 SMS Communication Policy

Taekwondo University may collect your mobile phone number when you voluntarily provide it through class registrations, appointment scheduling, event sign-ups, or other direct communication with us.

We use your phone number only to send important text message reminders related to:

- Scheduled classes or appointments
- School announcements
- Upcoming events or schedule changes
{: .pl-3}

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your phone number or personal information with third parties.

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying **STOP** to any text message you receive from us. After opting out, you will no longer receive text reminders unless you choose to re-subscribe.

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

If you experience difficulty with SMS opt status, please contact us directly at <a href="mailto:brumley@tkduniv.com" style="color: blue; text-decoration: underline;">brumley@tkduniv.com</a>, and we will make every effort to provide the requested information through an alternative method.
