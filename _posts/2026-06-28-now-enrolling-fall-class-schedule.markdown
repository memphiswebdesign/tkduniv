Our fall class schedule is officially open for enrollment! Whether your family is joining us for the first time or continuing training from summer, now's a great time to get on the calendar before class times fill up.

## What's new this fall

We've refreshed class times across all three of our programs to better fit school-year schedules:

- **Little Warriors** (ages 4-6) — additional after-school time slots
- **Kids Taekwondo** (ages 7-12) — expanded evening availability
- **Teen / Adult** (13+) — new early-evening class for busy schedules

## Why enroll early

Classes are grouped by age and skill level to keep group sizes manageable, which means popular time slots do fill up as the school year approaches. Enrolling early guarantees your family gets the schedule that works best for you.

Full details on class days and times are on our [schedule page](/schedule).

**Not sure which program fits your family?** [Schedule a free intro class](/free) and we'll help you find the right fit before you commit to anything.
